Welcome to the **Artificial Intelligence for Business Lab** repository!

2. **Marketer to Machine:** Develop level-2 Marketer-to-Machine (M2M) Scale of intelligent automation to personalize business email based on user preferences and interests (Extension to Exp. No.1). <br>

5. **AI and Advertisement:** Develop AI-powered programmatic advertising by using cookies and collecting user data. <br>
